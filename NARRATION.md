# Narration Draft

## Prototype Voiceover

`0s - 4s`
Here is a visual proof of the Pythagorean theorem using the exact same triangle, copied four times.

`4s - 10s`
Start with a right triangle. Its legs are `a` and `b`, and its longest side is `c`.

`10s - 24s`
Now place four copies inside a big square whose side length is `a + b`.
The empty space in the middle is a tilted square, and each side of that inner square is `c`.
So its area is `c squared`.

`24s - 40s`
Next, rearrange the same four triangles without changing the outer square.
Now the empty space is split into two squares instead: one with area `a squared`, and one with area `b squared`.

`40s - 52s`
The outer square stayed the same.
The four triangles stayed the same.
So the leftover area must also stay the same.

`52s - 60s`
That means `a squared plus b squared equals c squared`.

## Delivery Notes

- Keep the read energetic but not rushed.
- Pause slightly before the final equation.
- Let the rearrangement beat breathe so the viewer can track the pieces.
